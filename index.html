About

Gilson Design is the playground and personal portfolio of Milwaukee based designer, Adam Gilson. I’m a web designer with over ten years’ experience building websites for varied clients and industries. From digital strategy to rich application design, I can offer many services that can help any business improve their identity in the digital space. I love good typography, responsive designer, and I’ll always favour usability over needless decoration. I currently work at Zywave, where I get to work with some talented people to build software that help make the world a better place.

This is site has been designed & built responsively by hand using Dreamweaver, Notpad++, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Google Chrome.
It was designed with HTML5 & CSS3 in mind using LESS, with a little help from the Twitter Bootrap front-end framework to help me speed up my process.
Type is set in Proxima Nova & FF Meta Serif Web Pro, served by Typekit.
