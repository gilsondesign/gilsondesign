About

Gilson Design is the playground and personal portfolio of Milwaukee based designer, Adam Gilson. I’m a designer with over ten years experience building websites, providing branding and creating custom print pieces for various clients and industries. From strategy to rich website design, I offer many services that can help. Responsive design is a passion of mine and I’ll always favor usability over needless decoration. I currently work at Zywave, where I get to collaborate with talented people building software for the insurance and financial industries.

Hi, I'm Adam. Some super serious yet witty text goes here.

Where

Milwaukee is where I live, but city limits don't define my work. The world is well connected and I am happy to provide help anyway you prefer. Working through the project face to face, or online . . . that's up to you.

When

Communication is key. Thoroughly discussing project expectations and deadlines is a general guideline. Since I work a full time gig and usually have a side project or two on tap, time is sometimes a hot commodity. Get in touch now if you have something you'd like to get on my radar.

Why

I've worked in the web industry for ten years and done enough freelance to consider myself experienced. Putting the customer first has always been, and will always be, my philosohpy. I commit to making sure you are completely satisfied throughout the entire process.

How Much

I understand that every project requires a different mix of media and depth which can drastically affect project costs. I'd love to talk to you about the size and scope or your project and provide a quote. Quotes and advice are always free.

About This Site

This site has been designed & built responsively by hand using Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Google Chrome DevTools. It was designed with HTML5 & CSS3 in mind using LESS, with a little help from the Twitter Bootrap front-end framework to help speed up my process. Type is set in Droid Sans, served by Google Web Fonts.
